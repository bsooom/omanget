about us

The Global Energy and Technology Company is a joint-stock company established in 2022 in Oman. It is a leading company in the field of energy and clean energy technology. The company has extensive experience in these fields and has a specialized and qualified team of experts.The company has strategic partnerships with several Chinese, Indian, Saudi, and Jordanian companies in the fields of energy, clean energy, and technology.

areas of work

Health Services

The Global Energy and Technology Company offers comprehensive healthcare services, including primary and secondary medical care, as well as natural healing, rehabilitation, and home care services.

Energy

In terms of energy, the company works in both conventional and clean energy fields. In conventional energy, the company owns and operates the crude oil field "Al-Dhaww" and the oil shale field. In clean energy, the company invests in green fuel projects, such as green hydrogen and green ammonia.

Training

The company provides training services in a wide range of fields, including energy, clean energy, and technology. It also provides vocational and technical training services."

Aquaculture

The company invests in fish farming projects in fresh and salty waters."

Electric vehicle

The company invests in electric vehicle projects, including production and distribution. It also invests in the construction of electric vehicle charging stations.

Public investments

The company invests in diverse commercial and industrial projects.

Restaurants

The company invests in diverse commercial and industrial projects.

Drones

The company invests in projects for the development and use of drones. The company also provides imaging and monitoring services using drones.

Education

The company invests in projects of establishing and operating higher education institutions.

Import and Export

The company is active in the field of import and export, including import and export of petroleum and gas products, agricultural and industrial products.

Vision and Message

The company aims to be a leading player in the fields of energy, clean energy, and technology, and to contribute to achieving sustainable development in the Sultanate of Oman.

Achievements
